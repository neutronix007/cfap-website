UK Tax & Compliance for International Businesses

Expanding or operating in the UK as an international business comes with complex tax and compliance requirements. From corporate tax and VAT to payroll and reporting, non- compliance can lead to fines, penalties, and operational challenges. At CFAP UK Ltd, we specialise in providing practical UK tax and compliance support for international businesses, helping you navigate HMRC rules, optimise your tax position, and stay fully compliant.

Who Needs Our Services?

  1. Overseas SMEs and corporations establishing operations in the UK
  2. Companies looking to understand UK tax obligations for subsidiaries or branches
  3. Firms seeking assistance with accounting systems, reporting, and HMRC liaison
  4. International businesses wanting strategic guidance while minimising compliance risk

Our Services for International Businesses Include

  1. Corporate Tax Compliance: Ensure your UK entity meets all corporate tax obligations.
  2. VAT Compliance: Accurate VAT registration, reporting, and submissions.
  3. PAYE & Payroll Compliance: Manage UK employee taxes and contributions correctly.
  4. Accounting System Setup: Implement compliant, efficient bookkeeping and reporting systems.
  5. Making Tax Digital (MTD) Support: Ensure digital records meet HMRC standards.
  6. HMRC Liaison & Audit Support: Representation and guidance during inspections or queries.
  7. Ongoing Compliance Monitoring: Keep your business up to date with UK tax law and regulatory changes.

Why Choose CFAP UK Ltd?

  1. UK Tax Expertise: In-depth knowledge of UK tax law and compliance for international businesses.
  2. Tailored Solutions: We provide guidance suited to your business structure and goals.
  3. Smooth Compliance Transition: We help overseas businesses integrate into the UK regulatory environment with minimal disruption.
  4. Risk Mitigation: Avoid HMRC penalties and ensure accurate reporting.
  5. Strategic Guidance: Beyond compliance, we advise on best practices for sustainable UK operations.
