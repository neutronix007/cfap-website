Get to know about our company

CFAP UK Ltd is a UK-based accountancy, tax, and compliance firm supporting UK SMEs, international SMEs, and individual taxpayers. The company was incorporated on 24 February 2022 and is licensed to trade in 2025 by ICB.

We provide practical accountancy and tax consultancy to clients navigating the UK tax system, including self-assessment, corporation tax, and Making Tax Digital (MTD) requirements. Our work focuses on helping businesses and individuals remain compliant while making informed financial decisions.

CFAP UK Ltd takes a consultative approach, working closely with clients to understand their circumstances and provide clear guidance on accountancy, tax planning, compliance system setup, and HMRC obligations. We support both UK-based operations and international businesses engaging with the UK market.
